## 1. 男性性生理（Male Sexual Physiology）

### 1.1 Journal of Sexual Medicine

- 出版国家： Netherlands (originally, now under Oxford University Press, UK-based operations)
- ISSN： 1743-6095 (print), 1743-6109 (online)
- 简单介绍
  > This is a leading multidisciplinary journal focusing on basic science and clinical research in male and female sexual function and dysfunction, including physiology, pharmacology, and clinical management. It has a high impact factor of approximately 3.3 and is the official journal of the International Society for Sexual Medicine.
- 发表过的若干篇最著名的论文
  - The International Index of Erectile Function (IIEF): A Multidimensional Scale for Assessment of Erectile Dysfunction `国际勃起功能指数（IIEF）：评估勃起功能障碍的多维度量表`
  - Sildenafil Citrate (Viagra) for the Treatment of Erectile Dysfunction `西地那非柠檬酸盐（伟哥）用于治疗勃起功能障碍`
  - Epidemiology of Erectile Dysfunction: The Massachusetts Male Aging Study `勃起功能障碍的流行病学：马萨诸塞男性衰老研究`

### 1.2. Andrology

- 出版国家： United States
- ISSN： 2047-2919 (print), 2047-2927 (online)
- 简单介绍
  > This journal publishes research on male reproductive health, including physiology, endocrinology, and clinical aspects of andrology. It is co-published by the American Society of Andrology and the European Academy of Andrology, with an impact factor of about 3.4.
- 发表过的若干篇最著名的论文
  - The Role of Oxidative Stress in Male Infertility `氧化应激在男性不育中的作用`
  - Testosterone Replacement Therapy in Hypogonadal Men `低促性腺激素男性睾酮替代疗法`
  - Sperm DNA Fragmentation and Its Clinical Relevance `精子DNA碎片及其临床相关性`
